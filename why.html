WHY IT'S INTERESTING FOR ME

There's a many reasons for me to be interested in this career, one of them will be that I love to draw things and making them move is like bring art to live. Also thing is that a formal degree is not a necessity to become an animator. Many animators are self-taught or have completed alternative education programs. might be the reason for someone but mostly not for me because I anyway would like to get a digree. Good salary also the reason, many websites says something like this "Entry-level animators can expect to earn between $40,000 to $60,000 per year, while experienced animators and directors can earn upwards of $100,000 annually." But if being honest I think when you will start it's really hard to find work, just to get any money. That's exzactly why you want to start building portfolio before you'll try to find work or costumers. Portfolio will show skills you already have and your style of work, also it may help you to know how much does it takes to do different
