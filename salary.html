ANIMATOR SALARY

Depending on where you live and where you work salary for animator may be different. For Example The salary for 2D animators in the U.S. varies significantly, with average annual salaries ranging from about $58,000 to over $80,000, depending on experience, location, and employer. Entry-level positions may start lower, while experienced professionals can earn upwards of $100,000 per year, especially in high-paying sectors like media and communication. A 2D animator's salary in Japan varies, but annual averages can range from approximately ¥3 million to over ¥7 million(about $45,000), with some sources citing a wider range of ¥2(about $13,000) to ¥4 million(about $26,000) or even higher for senior roles. Monthly pay for entry-level positions is often lower, around ¥200,000-¥250,000($1,288-$1,610), while experienced artists can earn ¥350,000-¥450,000($2,255-$2,900) or more per month.
